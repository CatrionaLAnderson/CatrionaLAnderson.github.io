Overview

weCare is a well-being app developed as part of my Software Engineering project at Queen Mary University. The app was designed to help employees maintain a healthy work-life balance through fitness tracking, diet plans, and mental wellness resources.

Key Responsibilities & Skills

  • Leadership: Managed a team of 6, overseeing task allocations and deadlines.
  • UI/UX Design: Created high-fidelity prototypes in Figma to enhance the user experience.
  • App Development: Developed the fitness and diet sections, ensuring seamless front-end and back-end integration.
  • Presentation Skills: Delivered a final product pitch to an FDMgroup recruiter, demonstrating its business impact.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • Figma (UI/UX Design)
  • React Native (Front-end Development)
  • Node.js & Firebase (Back-end)
  • Agile Scrum Methodology
